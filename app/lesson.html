Welcome to Is This a Phish?™

In these lessons, you'll follow a person's journey as they advance in their career, encountering phishing emails along the way.

At the start, they're just a student, but as they progress, the phishing attempts become increasingly sophisticated.


How to Complete Lessons

You'll receive emails (don't worry, they're completely safe) and decide whether each one is a Phish or Legit email.

The tutorial lessons will teach you the basics of phishing, but as the difficulty increases, you'll need to rely on your own skills.


Reminder

Is This a Phish?™ was designed for people with no prior knowledge of cybersecurity.

It's not a replacement for established educational content or personal research.

You are encouraged to use Is This a Phish?™ alongside your own research.

Each lesson covers a different topic and provides sources for you to further your learning.
